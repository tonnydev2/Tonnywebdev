Bouncing Ball Physics Simulation

A realistic simulation of bouncing balls with adjustable physics parameters

Physics Principles Applied:
  • Blue Ball: Draggable with touch - dragging sets initial velocity
  • Green Ball: Perfectly elastic (restitution = 1.0)
  • Gravity: Constant downward acceleration acting on both balls
  • Restitution: Energy retained after each bounce
  • Air Resistance: Velocity-dependent damping that slows balls over time
  • Collision Detection: Full border collision with all four walls
