Pool Game Instructions

  • First shot is open - the first ball pocketed determines your color
  • Pocket yellow or red balls based on your assigned color
  • Fouls give the opponent two free turns
  • Black ball must be pocketed last to win
  • Pocketing the black ball early results in a loss
  • Use the aiming wheel to aim your shot
  • Adjust the power slider to control shot strength
  • Click "Take Shot" to execute your shot
