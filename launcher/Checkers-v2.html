How to Play Checkers

  • Red moves first, then players alternate turns
  • Move your piece diagonally to an adjacent dark square
  • Jump over your opponent's pieces to capture them
  • When a piece reaches the opposite end, it becomes a King
  • Kings can move and jump diagonally in any direction
  • Kings can also move multiple squares in a diagonal line
  • The player who captures all opponent pieces wins
  • In Human vs Computer mode, you play as Red against the AI (Black)
