Sticker Lottery Game

Test your luck and win coins!

How to Play

1. Click on a sticker to select it

2. Press START to begin the animation (costs 5 coins)

3. If the game stops on the exact sticker you selected, you win 50 coins!

4. If the game stops on the inverted version of your selected sticker, you win 10 coins!

5. No coins for any other outcome

6. If you run out of coins, wait 60 seconds to get 10 free coins!
