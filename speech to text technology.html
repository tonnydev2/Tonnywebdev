How to Use

  • Click the microphone button to start voice recognition
  • Speak clearly at a normal volume
  • Say letters (A to Z), numbers (0 to 9), or any of these symbols:
Period (.)
Comma (,)
Question Mark (?)
Exclamation Mark (!)
Colon (:)
Semicolon (;)
Hyphen (-)
Space
New Line
